What Vayu-Rescue Does

01 / 06
Autonomous Navigation

AI-powered flight stack using GPS, LiDAR, and visual odometry for fully autonomous navigation in GPS-denied and weather-challenged disaster zones — no human pilot required.

02 / 06
Thermal Victim Detection

Onboard thermal imaging with YOLO-based human detection models identify survivors beneath debris and in low-visibility environments with real-time AI classification.

03 / 06
Modular Payload System

Hot-swappable payloads for medical kits, water pouches, communication beacons, and sensor packages — configurable per mission type in under 3 minutes.

04 / 06
Last-Mile Aid Delivery

Precision GPS-guided payload drop or winch-delivery to survivors in inaccessible terrain — bridging the gap where ground teams and helicopters cannot reach.

05 / 06
Resilient Comms

Mesh-networking between drones and satellite uplink fallback ensure uninterrupted C2 communications in areas where cell towers and infrastructure are destroyed.

06 / 06
Disaster Zone Mapping

Real-time photogrammetric 3D mapping generates actionable disaster zone maps for rescue coordinators — updating every 10 minutes with AI-annotated hazard zones.

⚡ 100% OFFLINE · Uses Spatial Pyramid Histogram + Local Binary Patterns + Sobel Edge Gradients — matches facial structure and texture, not background colors. Works on file:// without internet.
